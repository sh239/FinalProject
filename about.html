About American Fried Chicken

At American Fried Chicken, we bring you the crunchiest and most flavorful fried chicken around. In the summer of 2005 two Bangladeshi immigrants who migrated to the United States to provide a better life for their family opened about American Fried Chicken.

Working day jobs by day and testing recipes by night, both partner finally opened a the corner shop called American Fried Chicken in 2005. It started with a tiny kitchen, a handful of menu items, and a whole lot of hustle. But word spread quickly—the chicken was unlike anything else. It was crispier, juicier, and packed with a flavor that lingered long after the last bite. We are proud to be a locally loved brand with a growing fanbase.

Over the years, the restaurant has remained a family-run business with sons, siblings, friends, uncles, and cousins all pitching in to keep the spirit alive. What started as a humble dream has now become a staple of the community—a place where locals gather not just for food, but for family, comfort, and culture.

Even today, American Fried Chicken is proud to serve the same mouthwatering meals made with love, laughter, and legacy. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch or ordering a feast for the whole crew, you're getting more than food—you're tasting a family's journey, one crispy bite at a time.
